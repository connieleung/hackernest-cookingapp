    Ingredients

      A

    • 1/4 c sugar
    • 2 1/2 c flour
    • 4 tsps baking powder
    • 1/4 tsp baking soda

      • B

    • 1 c sour cream
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 tsp vanilla

    • Add Mix

      • Add B mixture to A mixture
      • Mix until moistened
      • Turn onto floured surface

    • Knead

      Knead 19 times

    • Roll

      Onto about a 9 inch square

    • Cut

      • Cut with a glass dipped in flour
      • Place on UNGREASED cookie sheet

    • Bake

      350°F 12 minutes or until golden brown
